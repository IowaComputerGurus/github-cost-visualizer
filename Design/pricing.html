Simultaneous Meetings

We assume that the Business plan will have unlimited ability to have simultaneous meetings; enforcement of this will ONLY be on the time that the meeting was scheduled. MegaMeeting may allow a meeting to occur outside of the scheduled start time, any restriction of this manner will need to be validated with MegaMeeting.

Cloud Storage

We need to validate this as a yes/no option within the MegaMeeting API; it seems to be an option but needs confirmation.

Whitelabel Meetings

Whitelabel functionality will be limited to allowing users to customize the logos & options defined within MegaMeeting for an account, we will NOT white-label any of the developed solution, or notifications within the platform. (Users scheduling white-label mettings will need to send their own email notifications to attendees, to ensure proper white-label functionality)

Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan will be implemented as a manually assigned subscription type within the Chargify system, and will require the Customer to assign the subscription type of a user account. Users will NOT be able to select the Enterprise plan for a trial.
